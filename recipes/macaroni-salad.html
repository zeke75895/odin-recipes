Classic Macaroni Salad

Description

Classic macaroni salad is a creamy, flavorful side dish perfect for picnics, potlucks, and barbecues. Made with tender macaroni, crisp celery, onion, and bell pepper, this recipe gets its rich taste from a tangy-sweet dressing of mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, and a touch of sugar. Optional add-ins like grated carrots and pimentos bring extra color and texture, while simple seasonings like salt and pepper let the fresh ingredients shine. Best when chilled for at least four hours—or even made a day ahead—this salad allows the flavors to meld beautifully, making it a reliable crowd-pleaser. Praise from home cooks highlights its versatility, with many adjusting the sugar, swapping seasonings, or skipping certain veggies to suit their tastes. Whether served alongside grilled favorites or as a standout dish at family gatherings, this macaroni salad is so beloved that some reviewers joke about being asked to bring it to every event. Stored properly, it stays fresh in the fridge for up to five days—though it’s so delicious, it rarely lasts that long!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2/3 cup white sugar, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons chopped pimento peppers (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook macaroni pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  3. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and macaroni pasta together in a large bowl.
  4. Stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, and pimentos.
  5. Refrigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
