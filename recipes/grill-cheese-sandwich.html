Grill Cheese Sandwich

Description

The classic grilled cheese sandwich is a timeless comfort food that’s simple to make yet endlessly satisfying. This AllRecipes favorite uses just a few basic ingredients—soft white bread, buttery American or Cheddar cheese, and golden butter crisped to perfection in a hot skillet. The method is straightforward: butter the bread, layer the cheese, and grill until each side turns a toasty brown while the cheese melts into gooey perfection. Whether you stick with the traditional Cheddar or experiment with gourmet cheeses like Gruyère or Brie, this sandwich is a versatile crowd-pleaser. Perfect for a quick lunch or a cozy dinner paired with tomato soup, this grilled cheese recipe is foolproof and adaptable. Home cooks love its simplicity, while foodies appreciate the room for creativity—swapping in artisanal breads, adding a sprinkle of Parmesan for extra flavor, or even using mayonnaise instead of butter for a crispier finish. With a prep time of just five minutes and a total cook time under 15, it’s no wonder this recipe earns rave reviews for being both nostalgic and deliciously reliable.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  4. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese. Serve and enjoy!
