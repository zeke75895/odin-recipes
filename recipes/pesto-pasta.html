Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a quick, flavorful dish perfect for busy weeknights, ready in just 15 minutes. Made with your choice of pasta, a rich basil pesto sauce, and topped with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, this recipe is both simple and satisfying. Sautéed onions add depth of flavor, while salt and pepper enhance the vibrant taste of the pesto. Easily customizable, you can add proteins like chicken, sausage, or chickpeas, or serve it alongside crusty bread, a crisp Caesar salad, or balsamic bruschetta for a complete meal. Praise from the Allrecipes community highlights its versatility—many reviewers suggest doubling the sauce or adding extras like garlic, cherry tomatoes, or feta cheese for extra flair. Whether you stick to the basics or get creative with mix-ins, this pesto pasta is a reliable go-to for a delicious, fuss-free dinner. Leftovers store well in the fridge for up to five days, making it a great option for meal prep. Quick, easy, and packed with fresh flavors, it’s no wonder this dish is a favorite among home cooks.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons pesto or more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Stir in pasta and return to a boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and transfer into a large bowl.
  3. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in pesto, salt, and pepper until warmed through.
  5. Add pesto mixture to hot pasta; stir in grated cheese and toss well to coat.
